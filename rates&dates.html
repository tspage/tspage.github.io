Days Open & Center's Hours

Here at Dry Oar we believe that friends and families should be able to spend quality time together any and every day of the week! We are open Monday-Saturday from 8am-8pm (Sunday closed due to the Sabbath Day).

We are a functioning facility year round because of cleaning and maintaining the parks and rivers. But we are only open to the public during the months of March-september.

Rates+Sweet Deals
